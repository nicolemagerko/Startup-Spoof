What We Do

Singles Hide and Seek

We arrange monthly games of hide-and-go-seek with our members. Each month, one happily-single volunteers a local park or rubbish pit to have us all descend upon, platonically hiding and seeking in. As a bonus thank-you to our RedDeluxe members, anything found in the piles of trash that sparks their inner fire can be taken home at the end of the day and used as a platonic talking point for our after-seeking mixers.

Singles Long-Distance Telephone Calls

An exciting new perk for all of our members! We have recently secured a long-term partnership with Verizon Wireless, allowing each of our members to get in on our Lonely Hearts Club Family Plan Have a country you've been dying to call? Want to escape the hustle and bustle of single life and pretend you have an exotic lover? For an extra $5/month, we will provide you the number of another happily-single in our sister cities* for you to chat with to your heart's content!**

*Sister cities participating in the program include Bulgaria and Yugoslavia.

**If you end up starting a relationship with any other singles from our sister cities, you will be removed from the club and charged a matchmaking fee.
